Iconic Historical Places

Taj Mahal

A symbol of love, located in Agra. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan.

Red Fort

The residence of Mughal emperors, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Delhi.

Ajanta Caves

Ancient Buddhist cave monuments with stunning rock-cut sculptures.

Hawa Mahal

The Palace of Winds in Jaipur, known for its unique architecture.

Gateway of India

An arch monument in Mumbai, built to commemorate the visit of King George V.

Qutub Minar

The tallest brick minaret in the world, located in Delhi.
